Hobbies

Piano

Started playing at 8. Just like most kids, I don't practice . Nope, perhaps that why my scales always ended in single digits. Lets be honest, doing Czerny and Hanon, weeks after weeks, months after months and years after years, is a rather tedious job. Even though I knew of Chopin's existence, I didn't ask to play his pieces. This is dumb. VERY DUMB .

I found my passion to play the piano during my years studying in London. It's so difficult to find a free practice room in uni, especially when I'm not doing a music degree.

From getting only single digits in scales in exams to playing it everyday now. It's quite a big difference. Playing the instrument is definitely a great way to get away from the breeze of life.
